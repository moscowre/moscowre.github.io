MOSCOW RE

Company

The Moscow Reinsurance Company was founded in 1996 and was one of the first professional reinsurance companies in Russian Federation.

Portfolio

Moscow Re writes all lines of business. Gernerally the treaty business made up 60% of the written premium and facultative business about 40% respectively. In the portfolio there are prevailing (approximately 80%) the Property/Engineering and Motor risks.

The basic reinsurance segments of Moscow Re (property, CAR/EAR, marine hull, cargo and PA) are protected with the obligatory retrocession treaties, placed mainly among the foreign reinsurers with the rating not less than “A-“ of S&P’ scale or the similar ratings of the other international agencies.

Moscow Re has the long-lasting fruitful reinsurance interrelations with both the numerous national and CIS insurance companies.

Key financial data

In July 2015 S&P agency reaffirmed to “Moscow Re” its current rating “BB“ (stable outlook), and “ruAA”- according to the local country scale.

According to IFRS data for 2012 the total assets make up RUR 1 006 mln., profit after tax RUR 87,1 mln. 

In 2012 the written premium was RUR 1 101 mio and losses of RUR 793,9 mio.

Current investment portfolio (approx. RUR 1,6 bn) avarage rating is BBB-

Shareholders

In 2011 VTB Bank bought the only Moscow Re shareholder "Stolichnaya Strakhovaya Gruppa".
